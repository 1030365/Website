Q&A with an aspiring game developer*

The following is an interview with an aspiring game developer who wants to work for Nintendo someday.

INTERVIEW:

  1. Q: What makes you want to work at Nintendo as opposed to other companies?
  2. A: There are a few reasons, the first being that my favorite games are Nintendo IPs. Secondly, without naming names, I want to avoid companies that promote crunch culture. A lot of video game companies do it, but Nintendo is known to avoid it.
  3. Q: What are your favorite games?
  4. A: There are quite a few I'd like to mention; Minecraft has had the most impact on my life. I think that game is expertly designed to help kids discover what they like, and it did exactly that for me. I likely wouldn't be on a programming path without that game. Some other games I love are Terraria, Undertale, Super Smash Bros Ultimate, and Beatsaber. My favorite game is The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild.
  5. Q: What game do you think has the best soundtrack?
  6. A: There are a lot of games with soundtracks I love. To name some of my favorites, Sonic Mania, Undertale, Ori and the Blind Forest, Cadence of Hyrule, Hyrule Warriors: Age of Calamity, but the video game I think has the best soundtrack is Terraria.
  7. Q: Last question, what games do you hope to play soon?
  8. A: For me, the games currently out that I'd like to play soon are Ori and the Will of the Wisps, Sonic Colors Ultimate, WarioWare: get it Together, Metroid Dread, and the Legend of Zelda: Skyward Sword.

*Interviewer was also Interviewee
