Top 5 favorite video game soundtracks!

This is a list of my top five favorite video game OST's. The criteria for a game to be eligable are the following:

  • The music is judged in modern context, regardless of the date of release. Any songs on weaker hardware will not be judged on how good I think it was for it's time.
  • Songs must be original, with a few exceptions.
    • Remixes are only allowed if it is a remix of a song from a game in the same series, meaning Smash Ultimate is not elidgable, but Pokemon Remakes are. Spinoffs will count as "same series," even if it isn't made by the same developer.
    • Songs that contain pieces of other music without being remixes are elidgable
  • If these requirements are not met, the game cannot be on the list, but can be an honorable mention

#5: Pokemon Omega Ruby & Alpha Saphire

Pokemon games almost always have amazing soundtracks. Regardless of the quality of the games, the soundtracks are almost always excellent, consistently containing multiple songs that contend for a spot on my favorites list.
