Cheatsheet for HTML

Special Characters

Special characters include:

  • The "&" symbol, made with &amp;
  • The "<" and ">" symbol, made with &lt; and &gt; respectively
  • A non-breaking space is made with &nbsp;

Code

<p>Special characters include:</p>

<ul>
<li>The "&amp;" symbol, made with &amp;amp;</li>
<li>The "&lt;" and "&gt;" symbol, made with &amp;lt; and &amp;gt; respectively</li>
<li>A non-breaking space is made with &amp;nbsp;</li>

</ul>

Image Links

You can make images with
<img src="file path" alt="backup name">
For example:
<img src="images/DogByDoor.JPG" alt="DogOnBed">
results in:


You can make links with
<a href="link">Link Text</a>
For example:
<a href="http://eelslap.com/">Eel Slap</a>
Results in:
Eel Slap

To make an image that is also a link, you can combine these two things like so:
<a href="link"><img src="file path" alt="backup name"></a>
For example:
<a href="http://eelslap.com/"><img src="images/EEL.jpeg" alt="Eel"></a>
Results in:
